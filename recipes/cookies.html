Cranberry Pumpkin Cookies

Description

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F and grease two cookie sheets. Beat brown and white sugar and butter. Add an egg and beat until incorporated. Combine flour and baking soda in a separate bowl and stir mixture into the butter mixture until combined. Fold in cranberries and white chocolate chips. Drop batter onto prepared cookies sheets. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes.
